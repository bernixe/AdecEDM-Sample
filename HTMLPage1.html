Empowering Multi-Stage Careers

We are proud to announce the launch of WISE (Workplace Inclusion & Support for the Experienced) by Adecco.

In collaboration with the Alliance for Action on Empowering Multi-Stage Careers for Mature Workers (AfA-EMW), WISE is dedicated to co-creating real-work solutions that make Singaporean workplaces more age-friendly.

Are you a Singaporean Citizen or PR aged 40+?

WISE aims to enhance your job-seeking experience through specialized skills matching, lifelong learning opportunities, and dedicated career support.
