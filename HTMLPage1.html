Empowering Multi-Stage Careers

A partnership with MOM, NTUC, and SNEF

We are excited to share the launch of WISE (Workplace Inclusion & Support for the Experienced). In collaboration with the Alliance for Action on Empowering Multi-Stage Careers for Mature Workers (AfA-EMW), we are prototyping real-work solutions to make Singaporean workplaces more age-friendly.

WISE aims to support local Singaporean citizens and permanent residents aged 40 and above through skills matching, lifelong learning, and enhanced job seeking support.
